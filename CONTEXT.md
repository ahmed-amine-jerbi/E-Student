A platform where students can upload their homework and communicate with their class

Teachers can also upload and modify the time table for each class and subject

Tables: 
- Etudiants(Id, Nom, Prenom, Age, Class)
- Enseignants(Id, Nom, Prenom, Age)
- Class(Id, Libelle)
- Filiere(Id, Libelle)
- Matiere(Id, Libelle, Coefficient)
- Emploi()
